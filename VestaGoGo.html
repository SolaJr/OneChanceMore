VestaGoGo

A new way to navigate safely for blind and visually impaired civilians.


 

Role : Lead Coder / Product Designer

Client : Sketch Recognition Lab

Time : August 2018 - December 2018

This project is one of four research projects for Aggie Challenge I completed to apply modern research into product design. If you would like to see the other 4, please visit "Misc. Projects".

Navigation can be difficult when their are many things you need to keep your eyes and ears aware of. So we developed a mobile haptic vest and navigation system to allow people to navigate from place to place without obsturcting their sight or hearing.

Tools Used: Noodl (Framework), Figma, Andriod Studio (Java), Usablitity testing, and Ardunio.
